Pizza

Description

A dish of italian orgin consisting of flat, round base of dough baked with a topping of tomato sauce and cheese

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato paste
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Start with a crust
  2. Add a sauce
  3. Add veggies if desired
  4. Add some meat
  5. Add cheese
  6. Bake at 450F
